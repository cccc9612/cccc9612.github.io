About Me...

My professional journey began as an assistant logistics manager, where I sharpened my analytical and organizational skills. This role deepened my understanding of project management and operational efficiency, forming a solid foundation that would prove invaluable in my subsequent career shift to software engineering.

Driven by a passion to understand and construct digital frameworks, I quickly embraced the world of coding. My transition leveraged the robust project management skills and operational insights gained in logistics, allowing me to excel in developing strategically sound and efficient software solutions.

I started with vanilla JavaScript to create dynamic web pages, a foundational step that led to advanced frameworks like React. Further exploration into Flask and Express provided a deep dive into backend development, enhancing my ability to deploy full-stack applications. Utilizing technologies such as JavaScript, Python, and SQL, I have completed three comprehensive projects, each bolstering my expertise in both front-end and back-end development.
